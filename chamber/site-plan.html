Lagos Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Lagos Chamber of Commerce - This name represents the city of Lagos, which is a major economic hub in Africa. The chamber will serve businesses in Lagos by providing networking opportunities, business resources, and community engagement.

Site Purpose

  • To promote local businesses by offering networking events, advertising opportunities, and professional development.
  • To provide a platform where entrepreneurs and investors can find relevant business information, statistics, and contact details.

Scenarios

  • What networking events will be available for business professionals this quarter?
  • Where can I find resources to help start a new business in Lagos?
  • What are the current economic growth trends in Lagos?

Color Schema

Primary Color: (#004080 - Dark Blue for headers and navigation)

Accent Color: (#FFD700 - Gold for buttons and highlights)
